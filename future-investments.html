Future Investments

Long-term opportunities in emerging tech, AI, robotics, quantum computing, and digital infrastructure. These ideas are framed as researched opinions, not recommendations, focusing on where structural demand could unlock outsized upside.

Nebius

Nebius is positioned as a growing force in cloud infrastructure and AI compute services, offering scalable, high-performance systems that support next-generation workloads. As demand for AI training, model deployment, and enterprise cloud solutions accelerates, Nebius stands to benefit from a structural tailwind in digital transformation. The stock currently trades at a discount relative to its long-term cloud adoption potential, giving long-term investors an attractive entry point into an expanding market.

BigBear.ai (BBAI)

BigBear.ai provides AI-driven analytics and decision-intelligence platforms used in defense, logistics, and industrial operations. With governments and large enterprises increasingly relying on predictive analytics to improve efficiency and security, BigBear.ai offers real technological value. Despite meaningful long-term prospects, the stock is undervalued due to short-term revenue fluctuations. Its discounted valuation creates a favorable long-term setup as adoption of AI-enabled decision systems grows.

Applied Digital (APLD)

Applied Digital builds high-capacity data infrastructure, powering AI workloads, high-performance computing systems, and cloud compute. As AI models grow dramatically in size and complexity, demand for specialized data centers surges—and Applied Digital is strategically expanding to meet it. The company is in a sector with massive future demand, yet its stock currently trades well below intrinsic value due to early-stage volatility, making the long-term outlook compelling.

Serve Robotics (SERV)

Serve Robotics is pioneering autonomous delivery solutions with scalable, low-cost sidewalk robots. As demand for last-mile automation increases across retail, food delivery, and urban logistics, Serve is positioned to capture a growing market that values cost efficiency and sustainability. Despite being early in its growth cycle, Serve trades significantly below its long-term potential, offering deep-value upside for patient investors.

Rigetti Computing (RGTI)

Rigetti is a major U.S. quantum computing innovator building scalable quantum processors targeting breakthroughs in optimization, research, and AI acceleration. Quantum computing remains in early development, but global enterprise and government spending is rapidly increasing. Rigetti’s technological roadmap and partnerships create meaningful long-term value, even though the stock price remains heavily discounted relative to future commercial quantum demand.
