Inflation

General rise in prices over time. A 5% annual rate means a basket that cost $100 last year costs $105 this year.

Interest Rate

The price of money. Policy rates influence loan, mortgage, and savings rates throughout the economy.

Yield Curve

Bond yields across maturities. Inversions (short > long) can signal growth concerns.
