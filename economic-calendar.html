Economic Calendar

Economic events shape market movement—interest rate decisions, employment reports, inflation updates, and global political announcements can all shift investor sentiment. This calendar provides a simple overview of major upcoming events so readers can anticipate the week ahead.

This visual highlights how major reports and policy meetings cluster across the month, helping investors plan around jobs data, inflation prints, and central bank decisions.

Date Event Description
Dec 6 U.S. Non-Farm Payrolls Key jobs report influencing expectations for future interest rate moves and overall economic strength.
Dec 11 CPI Inflation Report Measures changes in consumer prices and heavily influences central bank policy decisions.
Dec 18 FOMC Meeting Interest rate decision and updated projections on growth, inflation, and the policy path.
Dec 22 Consumer Sentiment Survey Highlights household confidence, spending intentions, and perceived job security.
